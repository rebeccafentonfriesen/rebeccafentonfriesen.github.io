PhD Candidate, Mechanical Engineering

Northwestern University

Biography

I’m Becca, a Mechanical Engineering PhD student working with Northwestern’s Surface Haptics research group. I currently work with variable friction displays, looking both at the underlying mechanisms of how to manipulate friction as well as how to use this technology to create compelling haptic experiences.

While my latest degree is in engineering, I spent several years before graduate school working with neuroscientists on Brain Machine Interfaces. Observing the crippling consequences of lack of proprioceptive and tactile feedback during supposedly simple motor tasks informs and motivates my work today.
